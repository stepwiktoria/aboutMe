Certified RapidResponse Contributor Level 1

Earners of the Certified RapidResponse Contributor Level 1 certification have a basic understanding of how to navigate in RapidResponse, select the appropriate resources with the correct parameters to obtain the desired information, manage scenarios, edit data, use forms and to collaborate with others.

Certified RapidResponse Author Level 1

Earners of the Certified RapidResponse Author Level 1 certification have a basic understanding of the data model, commonly used tables, how to create and edit basic: alerts, workbooks, worksheets, filters, hierarchies, forms, crosstab worksheets with minimal referencing to technical documentation and assistance from peers.

Certified RapidResponse Administrator Level 1

Earners of the Certified RapidResponse Admin Level 1 certification can demonstrate knowledge of RapidResponse system, user and data administration in support of On-Demand and On-Premise systems.

Certified RapidResponse Integration Level 1

Earners of the Certified RapidResponse Integration Level 1 certification will be responsible to take a newly installed instance of RapidResponse and perform the initial integration of customer data, which includes creating a customized data model, mapping the customer data into standard input and custom tables, and populating standard and customized tables. Use of integration utilities to import initial RapidResponse configuration settings is also required.
